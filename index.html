The Venue

Now Sapphire - an upscale, all-inclusive beachfront resort located half an hour south of Cancun.

The resort stretches along a lush tropical mangrove preserve on a 5,000 foot Caribbean coastline. With beautiful, clean white sand, this pristine beach is protected by the second largest coral reef formation in the world.

Discover an exquisitely private oceanfront wonder located next to the Riviera Maya. Mayan architecture, lush landscaping and resplendent ocean views create an unforgettably luxurious experience!
